Pride and Accomplishment

Pride and Accomplishment is a game about getting as much dopamine, achievements, and other stuff, as you can.

The fist thing you can do is buy generators.

Generators

These are the first things you buy in Pride and Accomplishment.
The costs start with 10 dopamine, and each generator bought multiplies production by 1.8, along with doubling the cost.
Their power can be increased via achievements.

Achievements

Achievements are basically what keep the game going past a certain point. Achievements are earned every order of magnitude of dopamine made.
They also multiply production by 1.01 for each one earned. Prestige can help continue earning them.

Prestige

Prestige is unlocked at e6 dopamine. When you prestige, everything is reset except for the achievement boost.
After prestiging, you can regain achievements and continue increasing the boost.
The boost that remains is due to banked achievements, which will be used to buy upgrades in a future update.
